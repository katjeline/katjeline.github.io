Bedlington Terrier

The Bedlington Terrier looks suspiciously like a sheep. This dog breed originates from the eighteenth century and was used to catch mice.

Puli

This dog breed has dreadlocks that can go all the way to the groud and make him look like a mop. It protects him from cold and enemies.

Caucasian Ovcharka

The Caucasian Ovcharka is a huge dog breed that can weigh from 100 to 220 pounds. They are excellet guard dogs that are very protective over their family and territory. Nothing scares this big dog. They are easy to train, loyal and affectionate with their family and gentle with children.
